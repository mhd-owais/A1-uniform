About AONE

AONE Uniform Co. is a leading supplier of uniforms across educational institutions. Operating in Karachi, we use our extensive knowledge of uniform-making to help businesses dress their employees and strengthen their brands. By providing customized and personalized services, we ensure top-quality results by placing customer needs first and prioritizing their satisfaction. With over six decades of experience, our expertise lies in ethical bespoke uniform manufacturing and design with an emphasis on sustainable fabrics, trims, accessories, and packaging.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Create a holistic supply-side functioning for uniforms in Pakistan and become the customer's leading choice.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to catering to uniform needs across Karachi. We aim to redefine traditional monotony.

Quality Assurance

At AONE Uniform Co., you should only expect the best quality products, and we deliver just that. Our company follows high-quality standards to create unique work attire and staff uniforms for various sectors. Our products undergo extensive checks to ensure they meet and exceed international quality standards. Our QA personnel conduct final inspections on behalf of our customers to ensure the apparel looks good and meets client specifications.
